Common problems and practical fixes

  • APK will not install: Re-download the file, confirm the device supports it, and allow installs from the specific app that opened the file.
  • Sender and TV cannot connect: Put both devices on the same Wi-Fi, remove VPNs for setup, and reopen the app on both ends.
  • Transfer starts but fails: Check signal strength, keep the devices awake, and try a smaller file to rule out network instability.
  • App installs but behaves oddly: Restart the TV, clear app data if appropriate, and test whether an older or newer build works better.

When to stop forcing it

If you have retried the same APK, storage, and network steps more than once, move to the alternatives page instead of burning more time. A cloud folder, USB drive, or different file-transfer app can be the cleaner fix.
